Rules of Recitation

Learn Quran with Tajweed from Certified Native Teachers

Tajweed is the difference between simply reading the Quran and reciting it as it is meant to be recited. It governs the precise sound of each letter, the points of articulation, and the rhythm and pauses that carry meaning. Many of these details are almost impossible to spot in your own recitation, which is why a teacher who can hear every syllable and correct it in the moment is so valuable. Our certified Tajweed specialists do exactly that, one student at a time.

In a private session your teacher can pause on a single rule for as long as you need, then move on the moment it clicks. A child builds clean habits from the start, and an adult can finally iron out long-standing mistakes that were never picked up before. The correction is immediate, specific, and entirely focused on you.
