Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our online Arabic language classes, syllabus, and learning methods.

Do I need to know the Arabic alphabet to join this course?

No, this course is designed for all levels. For absolute beginners, we cover the basic alphabet and pronunciation rules first. For intermediate learners, we jump straight into grammar and syntax.

How long will it take to understand Quranic verses?

With 3 sessions a week, most students start understanding simple Quranic vocabulary and basic sentence structures within 3 to 6 months. Fluency in reading and translating complex structures takes longer.

What dialect or style is taught?

We focus on Classical Arabic (Fusha) and Quranic Arabic vocabulary/grammar, which is the key to understanding the Holy Quran and classical Islamic texts.

Are female tutors available for female students?

Yes, absolutely. We have highly experienced and certified female Arabic instructors for female students and kids.

Do you provide study materials?

Yes, we provide all the digital worksheets, vocabulary lists, and grammar guides in PDF format for free. Tutors display them during classes, and they are shared for home review.
