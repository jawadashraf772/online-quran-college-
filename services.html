1. Noorani Qaida Course

Learn Arabic alphabets, Makharij (letter origins), vowel symbols, and pronunciation rules from scratch.

  • Basic Arabic alphabet recognition and phonetic rules.
  • Understanding connecting letters, vowels, and stop signs.
  • Gradual progression from word-by-word reading to full verses.
View Noorani Qaida Syllabus & Details

This foundational course is designed to take a student from zero knowledge of the Arabic alphabet to reciting the Quran with fluency and accurate Tajweed rules.

Syllabus Highlights

  • Mufradat (Alphabets): Letter recognition and correct articulation points (Makharij).
  • Harakat & Tanween: Combining letters using vowel and double-vowel marks.
  • Tajweed Rules: Introduction to rules like Iqlab, Idgham, and Izhar.
  • Practical Reading: Practice reciting short verses of the Holy Quran.

Course Features

3. Hifz ul Quran Course

Structured program to memorize chapters or the entire Quran with revision patterns to ensure long-term retention.

  • Custom plans (memorizing specific Juz or the entire Quran).
  • Systematic daily recitation and cumulative revision loops.
  • Regular oral quizzes and final Hifz assessment tests.
View Hifz Syllabus & Memorization Details

Our Hifz program is structured to help students memorize the Holy Quran (in parts or completely) using proven cognitive and traditional revision strategies.

Memorization Methods & Prep

  • Tajweed & Pronunciation: Absolute focus on correct letter articulation.
  • Tarteel & Murattal: Developing melodic and rhythmic repetition habits.
  • Cognitive Chunking: Splitting verses and sections for easier recall.
  • Guidance & Feedback: 1-on-1 review with highly qualified scholars.

Revision Structure

  • Sabaq: Daily new lesson memorization.
  • Sabqi: Revision of the last 7 days of memorization.
  • Manzil: Rotating older memorized sections to prevent forgetting.
