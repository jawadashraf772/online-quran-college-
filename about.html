Our Journey

Making Authentic Quranic Wisdom Globally Accessible

Founded in 2018, Online Quran College started with a small group of certified teachers and a big vision: to create a reliable virtual environment where students of all ages could receive high-quality, customized, one-on-one Quran instruction.

Over the years, we have expanded to serve thousands of families across 25+ countries. We recognize that every student learns at their own pace. By structuring customized study plans, matching students with gender-appropriate qualified teachers, and integrating digital tools, we help our learners build a strong and lasting connection to the Holy Quran.

Teacher Standards

Our Rigorous Tutor Selection Standards

We believe the quality of Quranic instruction depends heavily on the caliber and dedication of the tutor. That is why we maintain an extremely selective hiring process.

  • **Scholastic Degrees**: All tutors hold formal Islamic Studies and Quranic recitation degrees from recognized global institutions (such as Al-Azhar University).
  • **Recitation Ijazah**: Teachers must hold an authorized Ijazah (recitation chain of transmission) with correct Tajweed rules.
  • **Background Verification**: Every scholar undergoes a background check and teaching skill evaluation.
  • **Pedagogical Training**: Tutors receive regular coaching on virtual classrooms and engaging teaching methods for kids.
