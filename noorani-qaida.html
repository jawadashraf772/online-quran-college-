Learning Roadmap

What You Will Learn

Our course goes beyond simple alphabet recognition; it is a structured roadmap to mastering the intricacies of Quran recitation.

Arabic Alphabet & Pronunciation

Master the Makhraj (place of articulation) to ensure every letter is produced correctly from its proper source, setting the foundation for accurate recitation.

Harakat & Tajweed Rules

Learn essential foundational rules like Idgham, Iqlab, Ghunnah, and Qalqalah to achieve beautiful, rhythmic, and accurate recitation from the start.

Word & Sentence Construction

Gain the practical skills to combine Arabic letters into words and eventually read complete Quran verses with confidence and fluency.

Memorization & Etiquette

Build proficiency through the memorization of short Surahs from Juz Amma and learn the essential Adab (etiquette) of handling and reciting the Holy Quran.

Flexible Learning From Home

Study from the safety and comfort of your home. With class scheduling options available 24/7, we fit perfectly into your busy life. Sibling discounts and custom pacing allow kids and adults to learn at their own comfortable speed.

Single Arabic Letters (Mufradat)

Introduction to the 29 letters of the Arabic alphabet. Focus is placed on correct pronunciation, differentiating similar sounds, and mastering articulation points (Makharij).

  • Mastering letter names and identification
  • Pronouncing throat letters correctly
  • Developing correct mouth shapes for heavy and light sounds
  • Drills to build correct pronunciation foundation

Module 1 Objective

Accurately identify and articulate every Arabic letter in its isolated form.

Compound Letters (Murakkabat)

Learning how Arabic letters change shape when joined together. Students master identifying letters at the beginning, middle, and end of words.

  • Recognizing initial, medial, and final letter shapes
  • Understanding connected letter combinations
  • Interactive matching drills for kids
  • Smooth transition from single shapes to joined words

Module 2 Objective

Read connected letters within composite words without hesitation.

Vowels and Movements (Harakat & Tanween)

Introduction to sound movements: Fathah (a), Kasrah (i), and Dammah (u), as well as Tanween (double vowels / an, in, un). This sets up reading vocabulary.

  • Applying Fathah, Kasrah, and Dammah movements
  • Differentiating short vowels from silent shapes
  • Pronouncing Tanween sounds with correct nasalization
  • Practice spelling exercises (Haji)

Module 3 Objective

Correctly pronounce words with short vowel sounds and Tanween markings.

Advanced Pronunciation & Long Vowels

Mastering long vowels (Madd rules), letters with Sakoon (resting state), and letters with Tashdeed (doubled emphasis). Introduces structural Tajweed rules.

  • Prolonging vowels (Madd) for 2, 4, or 6 counts
  • Echo sounds (Qalqalah rules) for resting letters
  • Applying nasalization (Ghunnah) on doubled letters
  • Pronouncing combined vowels and doubling rules

Module 4 Objective

Read complex vocabulary with vowel extensions, doubling, and pauses.

Fluency Practice & Short Surahs

Connecting all learned rules to read full sentences from the Quran. Students practice basic Tajweed rules and begin memorizing short daily Surahs.

  • Reading multi-word sentences fluently
  • Practicing proper stopping (Waqf) rules
  • Memorizing short Surahs (Al-Fatihah, Al-Ikhlas, etc.)
  • Learning the etiquette (Adab) of Quran recitation

Module 5 Objective

Transition smoothly from Noorani Qaida to reciting the Holy Quran directly.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our online Noorani Qaida classes, tutors, and learning resources.

What is Noorani Qaida?

Noorani Qaida is a classical structured booklet designed to teach beginners the rules of reading Arabic and reciting the Holy Quran correctly. It is the gold standard foundation for learning makharij (articulation points) and basic pronunciation rules.

What is the minimum age for this course?

We recommend starting this course for children from 4.5 to 5 years old. However, this course is equally beneficial and recommended for adults who want to correct their pronunciation from scratch or learn to read Arabic words fluently.

How long does it take to complete the Qaida?

For children taking 3 classes per week, it typically takes 4 to 6 months to complete the Noorani Qaida with correct pronunciation rules. For adults, it is often faster depending on their practice hours and individual pace.

Do I need to buy any book?

No, you do not need to buy any physical books. We provide a high-quality digital copy of the Noorani Qaida that the tutor displays on screen. We also share downloadable PDF copies for offline home practice.

Can we select a female teacher?

Yes, absolutely. We have dedicated, experienced female scholars available for children and sisters. You can select your preference in the trial registration form, and we will match you accordingly.
