Rules of Recitation

Learn Quran Online with Tajweed from Certified Native Teachers

Tajweed refers to the set of rules that govern the correct pronunciation and recitation of the Quran. Reciting with proper Tajweed is not just a tradition but a responsibility, ensuring that every word is delivered with the precision and respect it deserves. At Online Quran College, our teachers are trained specialists in Tajweed who guide students through each rule in a clear, step-by-step manner covering pronunciation, articulation points, and recitation rhythm.

Our learn Quran online with Tajweed programme welcomes students at every stage. If you are an adult who has never formally studied Tajweed before, or a student looking to refine an already established recitation, our structured curriculum and regular feedback sessions give you everything you need to make real, measurable progress at your own pace.

Find answers to common questions about our online Quran classes in the UK.

Are your online Quran classes suitable for complete beginners in the UK?

Absolutely. Our online Quran classes for beginners start from the very foundation, covering the Arabic alphabet and Noorani Qaida, so no prior knowledge or experience is needed. Students of all ages and levels are welcome to join.

Yes. Online Quran College runs dedicated programmes for children and adults, each structured around the learning style, attention span, pace, and goals of that specific age group.

Are both male and female Quran teachers available?

Yes. We provide certified male and female Quran teachers. Families who prefer a female teacher for their daughter or for themselves can request one at the time of registration.

What is the difference between reading the Quran and learning the Quran with Tajweed?

Basic Quran reading focuses on recognizing Arabic letters and reading words correctly. Learning the Quran with Tajweed builds on this by teaching the precise rules of pronunciation, rhythm, and recitation that ensure the Quran is read exactly as it was revealed. Online Quran College offers both levels as part of its curriculum.

How do I start online Quran tuition with Online Quran College?

Simply register for a free trial class through our website. A member of our team will contact you to schedule your first session at a time and day that suits you. No payment is required for the trial class.

Is online Quran tuition as effective as attending in-person classes?

Yes. A private 1-on-1 online session with a qualified Quran teacher delivers the same level of personalized instruction as an in-person lesson, and often more so, because the student receives the teacher's full attention, sessions are recorded for review, and scheduling is entirely flexible around the student's life.

What courses does Online Quran College offer beyond basic Quran reading?
