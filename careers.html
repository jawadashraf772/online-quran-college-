Why Teach With Us?

At Online Quran College, we are committed to providing our faculty members with an environment that respects their dedication, supports their professional growth, and ensures flexible work hours.

  • Flexible Working Hours Choose shift hours that suit your timezone. Teach full-time or part-time.
  • Global Student Base Instruct students from the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Europe, and Middle East.
  • Competitive Remuneration Get timely, reliable monthly payouts with rewards for high student retention.
  • Advanced Digital Classroom Access state-of-the-art LMS dashboards, lesson schedules, and class progress trackers.

Academic & Technical Qualifications

We enforce rigorous selection standards to maintain educational quality. Candidates must fulfill the following:

  • Possession of a formal Recitation Ijazah with accurate Tajweed.
  • Degree in Islamic Studies, Arabic Literature, or equivalent certification.
  • Strong communication skills in English or Urdu for teaching non-native speakers.
  • Reliable high-speed fiber internet and backup power source.
  • Clear HD webcam, quality noise-canceling headset, and noise-free space.
