Foundational Qaida & Letter Mastery

Mastering Arabic alphabets, letter shapes, vowel movements (Harkat), and the core basics of reading Arabic words fluently. This sets the foundation for flawless reading.

  • Mastering Arabic alphabets & pronunciation
  • Connecting letters in words
  • Sound movements: Fathah, Kasrah, and Dammah (Harkat)
  • Basic spelling and reading fluency exercises

Module 1 Objective

Correctly identify and pronounce single and joined Arabic letters with correct vowel movements.

Essential Tajweed Rules

Introduction to the fundamental rules of recitation that bring rhythm and correct structure to reading. You will learn to hold, merge, and echo sounds as they were traditionally revealed.

  • Rules of Sakoon and Tashdeed (letter doubling)
  • Madd rules (Prolongation marks: 2, 4, or 6 counts)
  • Ghunnah (Nasal sound prolongation) and Waqf rules
  • Tanween rules (Izhar, Idgham, Iqlab, and Ikhfa)

Module 2 Objective

Recite verses applying doubling, joining, nasalization, and extension rules automatically.

Surah Recitation Proficiency

Progressive reading practice of the Quran, starting from the shorter Surahs of the last Juz (Juz 30 / Juz Amma) and systematically moving toward longer and more complex passages.

  • Fluid reading of Juz 30 Surahs (e.g., Al-Fatihah, Al-Ikhlas)
  • Connecting rules directly during active recitation
  • Correct pausing patterns and Waqf rules implementation
  • Reciting with appropriate rhythm and steady pace

Module 3 Objective

Fluently recite short Surahs from memory or reading with correct rhythm and stopping points.

Verses (Riyaat) Comprehension

Developing mastery over proper breathing, rhythm, and intonation, while beginning to grasp the underlying meanings and spiritual messages of the verses recited.

  • Breath control and breath distribution for long verses
  • Proper vocal intonation matching the theme of verses
  • Basic word-by-word meaning and context introduction
  • Developing emotional and spiritual presence during reading

Module 4 Objective

Acquire proper breath management while reciting, reading with reverence and understanding.

Daily Supplications & Masnoon Duas

Learning essential daily prayers and supplications (Duas) from the Sunnah to integrate blessings and Islamic manners into your daily routines.

  • Duas for sleeping, waking up, eating, and entering the home
  • Practical steps of Wudu (ablution) and Salah (prayer)
  • Short Azkar (remembrances) for morning and evening
  • Memorizing essential Masnoon Duas with translation

Module 5 Objective

Incorporate essential Islamic etiquettes, prayers, and Duas into daily family life.
